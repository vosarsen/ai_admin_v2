## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**CLEAR WINNER: adminai.tech**

**Key Reasoning:**
1. **Target Audience Alignment**: .tech communicates "business technology solution" while .dev signals "developer tool" - critical misalignment for non-technical business users
2. **Pricing Reality**: Both TLDs cost similar (~$10-20 first year), but .tech renewal ($40/yr) is actually 3-4x higher than .dev ($12/yr) - contradicts user's claim of "practically same"
3. **Semantic Clarity**: "Admin AI Tech" sounds like business automation; "Admin AI Dev" sounds like development API
4. **Flexibility**: .tech allows broader positioning; .dev pigeonholes as developer-focused product
5. **Market Positioning**: .tech has 300,000+ business adoptions with explicit business automation focus

**However, .dev has advantages:**
- Google-backed infrastructure (more stable)
- Mandatory HTTPS (SEO benefit)
- Lower long-term cost ($12/yr vs $40/yr renewal)
- Developer credibility if pivoting to API/SDK later

**Critical Issue:** User stated prices are "practically the same" but research shows .tech renewal is **$40/yr vs .dev $12/yr** - a significant 233% difference that contradicts this assumption.

## 4. SEO COMPARISON

### Traditional SEO Factors

**Impact of TLD on SEO:**
- Google treats all gTLDs equally for ranking purposes (confirmed by Google)
- No inherent SEO advantage of .com vs .dev vs .tech
- Domain name itself matters more than extension

**However, there ARE indirect SEO differences:**

**.dev SEO Advantages:**
- ✅ **Mandatory HTTPS:** All .dev sites require HTTPS (HSTS preload list)
- ✅ **Security ranking signal:** HTTPS is a confirmed Google ranking factor (since 2014)
- ✅ **Trust signals:** Padlock icon, reduced bounce rates from security
- ✅ **Better referral data:** HTTPS-to-HTTPS preserves referral data
- ✅ **Enhanced analytics:** Google Search Console HTTPS reports
- ✅ **HTTP/2 support:** Performance optimization for faster loading

**.tech SEO Considerations:**
- ⚠️ **HTTPS recommended, not required:** Must manually configure SSL
- ✅ **No disadvantage if HTTPS implemented:** Same SEO benefits as .dev once configured
- ✅ **Broader semantic context:** "Tech" keyword in domain may help topical relevance
- ❌ **No built-in security enforcement:** Depends on implementation

### Page Speed & Performance

Both TLDs perform identically once HTTPS is configured:
- Modern SSL/TLS protocols are optimized (minimal performance impact)
- HTTP/2 improvements available on both
- DNS resolution speed depends on registrar/nameserver, not TLD

### CTR & User Behavior

**No specific data found comparing .dev vs .tech CTR**

General findings:
- Familiarity drives clicks (hence .com still dominates)
- Security signals (HTTPS padlock) improve CTR
- Semantic clarity helps ("tech" clearer than "dev" for general audiences)

**Winner on SEO:** .dev (built-in HTTPS enforcement gives slight edge, but marginal once .tech implements HTTPS)
